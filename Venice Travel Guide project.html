Discover Venice

Explore the beauty of the canals, art, and rich culture of Venice, Italy.

Top Attractions in Venice

Grand Canal

Experience Venice's main waterway with its stunning palaces and gondolas.

Ducal Palace

Admire Venetian Gothic architecture and rich history in this magnificent palace.

Rialto Bridge

The iconic bridge over the Grand Canal, perfect for shopping and sightseeing.

What to Eat in Venice

Squid Ink Pasta

A delicious Venetian seafood specialty that you must try!

Cichetti

Small Venetian tapas-like dishes perfect for sharing with friends.

Tiramisu

The classic Italian dessert – a sweet ending to any meal in Venice.

Travel Tips for Venice

Make the most of your visit to Venice with these tips:

  • Wear comfortable shoes – Venice's streets are best explored on foot.
  • Take a Vaporetto – it's Venice’s public water bus and offers fantastic views.
  • Visit early or late to avoid the crowds at major attractions.
  • Embrace the culture of "Caffè" – enjoy coffee at one of the many charming cafés.
